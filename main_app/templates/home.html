SnippetCollector

Are you a developer? A shortcut lover? Someone who just wants quick access to bits of their information? Introducing SnippetCollector!--an app where you can create 'snippets' of data and assign tags to them so that you can easily find them later via search bar!

NEW FEATURE: GROUPS! Tired of searching for a single-tense word, but not seeing results for other variations of it? Now you can create groups of tags! If you search for a tag that is part of a group, then your search will cleverly include all the tags of that group!
